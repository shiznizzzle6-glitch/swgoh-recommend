📋 Territory Battle — how to play each phase (Rise of the Empire)

Every unit can be used only once per phase, so do these in order — deploy last. Deploying first locks your best units out of the missions and platoons where your rewards actually come from. Tick as you go; reset when a new phase opens.

  • ⚔️ 1. Combat missions
    Clear every mission you can win with your best squads. Match the lane: 🔵 Light · 🟠 Mixed (any) · 🔴 Dark. This is where your gear & currency come from.
  • ✨ 2. Special missions (◎ nodes)
    Need a specific faction/GL squad — attempt any you can field for the biggest rewards and bonus stars.
  • 🚀 3. Fleet missions
    Ships only — a separate pool from characters, so they never compete. Clear what you can.
  • 🎖️ 4. Platoons
    Donate leftover units for guild-wide rewards — the platoon sections lower on this page show which you can fill. Never platoon a unit you still need for a mission.
  • 📦 5. Deploy — last
    Spread everyone still available across all open planets (the game sorts them by alignment). Stop each planet at ⭐⭐⭐ — GP past 3 stars is wasted.
  • One use per unit per phase — missions & platoons before deployment, always.
  • 3 stars is the cap per planet — don't pile your whole roster onto one; spread it.
  • Lanes: 🔴 Dark Side · 🟠 Mixed · 🔵 Light Side — units only deploy to their alignment.
  • 🔒 planets unlock as the guild stars the ones next to them — clearing yours opens more.
